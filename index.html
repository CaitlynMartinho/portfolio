About me

Hi, I’m Caitlyn Martinho - a developer and designer based in Pretoria. I’ve been working with code and design since I was 12, and I’m currently studying Interactive Media Design. I completed matric in 2023 at Hoërskool Warmbad and studied UI Design in 2024, where I developed a strong understanding of usability and visual design.

I enjoy puzzles, design and art, and exploring video games. My goal is to become a UI/UX designer and front-end developer so I can use my skills to make users’ lives easier and create meaningful impact through my design ideas.
